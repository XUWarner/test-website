What is Vertical Farming?

Last updated: 2026-02-19

Definition｜定义

Vertical farming refers to growing crops in vertically stacked layers under controlled environments (CEA).
垂直农业指在可控环境（CEA）下，通过多层结构种植作物。

Key components｜核心要素

  • LED grow lights｜植物生长灯
  • Hydroponics / Aeroponics｜水培/雾培
  • Climate control (temperature, humidity, CO₂)｜环境控制

My take｜我的理解

In land-scarce cities like Singapore, vertical farming can improve yield per square meter, but energy cost is a key constraint.
在新加坡这种土地稀缺城市，垂直农业能提高单位面积产量，但能耗成本往往是关键约束。
