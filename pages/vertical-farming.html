Key Features of Vertical Farming / 垂直农业的关键特征

  • Indoor or semi-indoor environment · 室内或半室内
  • Multi-layer racks · 多层种植架
  • Artificial lighting (LED) · 照明补光系统
  • Precise control of temperature, humidity, CO₂ concentration, light spectrum, nutrients · 精确控制温度、湿度、二氧化碳浓度、光谱、营养液等
