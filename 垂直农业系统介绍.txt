# Abstract
#介绍

This project presents a conceptual design of a smart vertical farming system for lettuce production under controlled environment agriculture (CEA).
该项目展示了在可控环境农业下的以生菜为样本的模型设计

The system integrates environmental sensing, a central intelligent control platform, and multiple subsystems including LED lighting, climate control, CO₂ regulation, fertigation, and pH monitoring.
A closed-loop feedback mechanism is used to dynamically adjust environmental parameters in order to improve yield stability, energy efficiency, and production consistency.
该系统结合了环境传感器、中控系统以及包括LED照明系统、温控系统、二氧化碳调节系统、水肥系统以及pH检测系统等在内的多种亚系统。采用了动态调节环境参数以完成环境检测和反馈调节的闭环控制，以提升产量稳定性、能效最高利用、生产持续性。

#Key Feature of vertical farming
#垂直农业的关键特征
1.Indoor or semi-indoor environment
1.室内或半室内
2.Multi-layer racks
2.多层种植架
3.Artificial lighting(LED)
3.照明补光系统
4.Precise control of temperature, humidity, carbon dioxide concentrantion, light spectrum, nutrients
4.精确控制温度、湿度、二氧化碳浓度、光谱、营养液等

vertical farming system depends on sensors, so loT is crucial.
垂直农业系统依赖于传感器，因此物联网十分重要

So the whole system works like:
整个系统的工作逻辑展示：
sensors collect environmental data --- Controller processes data ---Control logic decides actions --- Actuators change the environment
传感器收集环境信息 --- 控制器处理数据 --- 逻辑判断决定如何调整 --- 执行器调整参数改变环境


#Several Important parameters
#若干重要参数

Temperature
温度
Lettuce ideal：18~22℃
生菜理想生长温度：18~22℃

Humidity
湿度
Lettuce ideal: 50~70% RH
生菜理想生长湿度：50~70% RH

Carbon dioxide concentration
二氧化碳浓度
Higher carbon dioxide concentration means faster photosynthesis, but only effective when the light and nutrients are sufficient
高浓度二氧化碳促进光合作用效率，但要和水肥供应配合才能最大化生长效益

Light
光照
Light spectrum: red/blue ratio
光谱：红光或蓝光
