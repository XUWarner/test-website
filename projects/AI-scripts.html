AI Live Stream Interaction Scripts

AI-generated scripts for different live stream scenarios. · AI生成的直播互动话术方案。

AI直播互动话术

Last updated: 更新： 2026-02-22

SCENARIO 1

Opening Interaction · 直播开场互动

Version A: Directive Promotion
方案A：直接促销

Welcome everyone! We have limited-time discounts today. Click the coupon button now to get 20% OFF and don't miss today's best deals!

欢迎来到直播间！限时优惠不容错过！现在点击优惠券按钮，立享八折优惠，不要错过今天的爆款！

Version B: Interactive Trigger
方案B：互动后触发优惠

Hi everyone! Type "1" in the chat to unlock your exclusive coupon. Once we reach 100 messages, I'll release a special discount for you!

大家好～在公屏打 "1" 解锁专属优惠券！当互动达到 100 条，我马上给大家发一波限时福利！

Metrics: Engagement rate / CTR / Conversion rate · 评价指标：参与互动率 / 点击率 / 转化率

SCENARIO 2

Mid-stream Interaction · 流程中互动

Version A: Pain-point Diagnostic
方案A：痛点诊断（互动深度）

Quick check: do you face slow performance, high maintenance, or poor durability with similar products? Type "A" for slow performance, "B" for high maintenance, or "C" for poor durability. I'll show you how this product fixes your problem.

快速自测一下：用同类产品时，你更常遇到的是 A. 使用慢 / 效果差，B. 维护麻烦，还是 C. 不耐用？在公屏打 A / B / C，我马上告诉你这个产品是怎么解决对应问题的！

Metrics: 多选回复比例 · 讲解后商品点击率（CTR）· 对应痛点的转化率分布

Version B: Challenge-based Interaction
方案B：挑战互动（互动密度）

Let's do a quick challenge! If we get 50 messages in the next 30 seconds, I'll unlock a hidden feature and an extra bonus for you. Ready? Type "GO"!

来个小挑战！30 秒内公屏刷到 50 条消息，我就解锁一个隐藏功能 + 额外福利给大家！准备好了吗？在公屏打 "GO"！

Metrics: 30秒内互动量 · 达成挑战比例 · 解锁福利后的点击率/下单转化率

SCENARIO 3

Pre-discount Trigger · 价格公布前的转化引导

Version A: Countdown
方案A：倒计时

The special price will be released in 10 seconds! Type "ready" in the chat if you're ready to grab the deal!

专属价格 10 秒后公布！准备好的朋友在公屏打 "准备好了"，一起冲这波福利！

Version B: Qualification
方案B：条件触发

I'll release the discount only for those who interact with me. Type "deal" in the chat to unlock your exclusive price!

只有参与互动的朋友才能解锁专属价格，在公屏打 "冲"，我马上给你开通优惠资格！

Metrics: Interactions before showing price / Coupon collection rate / Conversion after showing price in 60s · 评价指标：价格公布前互动人数 / 优惠领取率 / 公布价格后一分钟内下单转化率

SCENARIO 4

FAQ Interaction · FAQ互动

Version A: Information-first
方案A：信息安抚

Many of you are asking about delivery and refund. Delivery takes 3–5 working days, and we support 7-day no-reason return. Type "ok" if this policy works for you!

很多朋友在问发货和售后问题，现在下单 3–5 个工作日发货，支持 7 天无理由退换。可以接受的朋友在公屏打 "OK"，我马上继续讲福利！

Version B: Social Proof
方案B：增强信心

Most buyers chose this product because of fast delivery and easy refund. Type "安心" if delivery and after-sales service are important to you!

很多下单的朋友最看重的就是发货快、售后省心，这些对你很重要的，在公屏打 "安心"！

Metrics: Interaction after FAQ / Repeat question rate / Conversion after FAQ in 3 minutes · 评价指标：FAQ后的互动率 / 相关问题重复提问率 / FAQ后三分钟内下单转化率
