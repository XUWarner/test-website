视觉识别检测产品案例

AI-based Visual Inspection Product for Manufacturing Quality Control

Designed an AI-based visual inspection product using deep learning models to identify and mark product defects in manufacturing scenarios. The system handles high-frequency, standardized preliminary inspection tasks, reducing manual labor while improving detection consistency and stability. Through human-machine collaboration mechanisms, manual review is retained for low-confidence scenarios to control quality risks. The solution addresses pain points of high manual inspection costs, fatigue-induced accuracy fluctuations, and shift-based quality variations.

设计了一款基于 AI 的视觉检测产品，通过深度学习模型对产品图像进行识别与缺陷标记。该系统承担高频、标准化的初筛检测任务，在降低人工工时的同时提升检测一致性与稳定性。通过人机协同机制在低置信度场景下保留人工复核，控制质量风险。解决了人工质检成本高、疲劳导致准确率波动、班次交接导致质量不稳定等痛点。

优化直播互动话术以提升用户互动率与转化率

Optimize Live-stream Interaction Strategy to Improve Engagement and Conversion

This project aims to optimize live-stream interaction scripts to improve user engagement and conversion. By designing an A/B test on different opening interaction strategies in the first 60 seconds of live streams, I leveraged LLM-generated scripts (AIGC) and defined key metrics such as engagement rate, CTR, and conversion rate to evaluate performance. Through simulated data analysis, the interactive-trigger strategy showed higher engagement and conversion, and further iterations were proposed to continuously optimize interaction effectiveness.

本项目旨在通过优化直播互动话术，提升用户互动率与转化率。围绕直播开场前 60 秒的互动策略设计 A/B 对照实验，结合大语言模型生成话术（AIGC），并以互动率、点击率和转化率作为评估指标进行分析。基于模拟数据结果，互动触发型策略在互动与转化上表现更优，并提出后续迭代方向以持续优化直播互动效果。

可控环境农业下的以生菜为样本的模型设计

A Conceptual Design of a Smart Vertical Farming System for Lettuce Production under Controlled Environment Agriculture (CEA)

The system integrates environmental sensing, a central intelligent control platform, and multiple subsystems including LED lighting, climate control, CO₂ regulation, fertigation, and pH monitoring. A closed-loop feedback mechanism is used to dynamically adjust environmental parameters in order to improve yield stability, energy efficiency, and production consistency.

该系统结合了环境传感器、中控系统以及包括LED照明系统、温控系统、二氧化碳调节系统、水肥系统以及pH检测系统等在内的多种亚系统。采用了动态调节环境参数以完成环境检测和反馈调节的闭环控制，以提升产量稳定性、能效最高利用、生产持续性。
